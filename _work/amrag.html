PROJECT

Let Yourself Go at American Rag

To celebrate the launch of the Fall '13 Campaign, Hudson hosted a special evening at American Rag. Guests and shoppers were treated to live music, craft beer from Glendale Brewery, handcrafted Hudson Whiskey cocktails, and freebies like custom Hudson totes and iPhone cases with the imagery from the campaign. Shoppers were also entered into a raffle for a Lakers game.
